Banana Pancakes

Description

Warm pancakes with your favorite toppings like syrup, fresh fruit, or a dollop of whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk (or more if needed)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil (for cooking)
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mash the Banana: In a bowl, mash the ripe banana with a fork until smooth with a few lumps remaining.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the mashed banana and mix well.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, combine the flour, sugar (if using), baking powder, and salt.
  5. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter may be a little lumpy, which is okayâ€”don't overmix!
  6. Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and add a little butter or oil. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface (about 2-3 minutes), then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
